# PRINCE2 Product Delivery Pack

Use this workflow when a team manager or delivery team needs to accept, execute, and deliver a work package under PRINCE2 controls.

## Inputs To Ask For

- Work package description
- Product descriptions and quality criteria
- Team capacity and delivery approach
- Dependencies and constraints
- Reporting cadence
- Quality review method
- Delivery acceptance route

## Output Structure

### 1. Work Package Acceptance
- Products to deliver
- Acceptance criteria
- Quality method
- Constraints
- Reporting expectations
- Assumptions and dependencies
- Acceptance recommendation

### 2. Team Delivery Plan
- Activities
- Owners
- Dates
- Quality checks
- Dependencies
- Escalation points

### 3. Checkpoint Report
- Period covered
- Products completed
- Products in progress
- Forecast completion
- Risks and issues
- Help needed

### 4. Quality Evidence
- Product
- Quality criteria
- Review method
- Result
- Defects or follow-up
- Approval status

### 5. Delivery Handover
- Products delivered
- Acceptance evidence
- Open defects or exceptions
- Operational notes
- Recommendation to accept, accept with conditions, or reject

## Quality Bar

- Do not accept vague work packages.
- Product quality criteria must be testable.
- Checkpoint reporting should be short and factual.
- Delivery is not complete until acceptance evidence is clear.
