# Product Requirements Blueprint Example

Use `product-requirements-blueprint` when a feature needs enough clarity for design, engineering, and stakeholders to align.

Prompt:

```text
Use product-requirements-blueprint for a customer onboarding dashboard.

Problem:
Customer success managers cannot see where new accounts are stuck during onboarding.

Users:
Customer success managers and onboarding specialists.

Success metrics:
- reduce time-to-first-value by 20%
- reduce onboarding escalations by 15%
- increase accounts completing setup in 14 days

Scope:
MVP dashboard with account status, setup steps, owner, risks, and next action.
```

Expected output:

- Problem statement
- Proposed solution
- User stories
- Functional and non-functional requirements
- Metrics
- Scope boundaries
- Open questions
